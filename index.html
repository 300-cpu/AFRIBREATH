Our Application Process

Embarking on a journey to transform tech training into real-world, our virtual Internship program follows a seamless process to empower students and enhance their tech skills

Below is a closer look at how our program works:

  • Recommendation

    Interns Join upon recommendations from a school, company or organization, ensuring a curated and quiality pool.

  • General Assessment

    We assess student resumes, Linkedin profiles and portfolios to gain thorough understanding of their background

  • Skills Assessment

    Each student undergoes a skills test,determining their fitness for the role and ensuring a match with the program
